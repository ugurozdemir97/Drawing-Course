  16. Shading practice part 1 - Shades

    Just like the line practice class, we also need a shading practice class before we actually start learning about shading. If you are a beginner you probably don't have enough muscle contol to keep your pen pressure stable and fill shapes without overflowing them. So in this class we will practice our pen pressure contol, and filling shapes without overflowing.

  17. Shading practice part 2 - Gradients

    32. LET'S LEARN HOW TO DRAW HUMANS (PART 2 – SECTION 2)

     We will learn how to draw humans from imagination. We will do gesture drawings, simplify human forms, learn the importance of memorizing and learn all the anatomy that we need to know as artists.

  32. Quick drawings

    In this class we will draw a lot of gesture drawings. To be able to draw humans from imagination, obviously the first thing you should do is drawing humans. I recommend you to draw whatever you want to learn hundreds of times in 10 to 20 minutes and after you start to understand it, try to draw it in faster times like 5 minutes, 2 minutes or even under 1 minute. Drawing slow and fast has their own benefits, so do both.
     Gesture drawings
     Gesture drawings

  33. Simplifying human forms

    You should simplify whatever you are learning and practice drawing it with simple forms. This will let you feel its form in 3D and remember complex structures easier.
